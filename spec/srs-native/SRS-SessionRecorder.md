# SRS - Native Session Recorder & Entity Dump

Native-specific requirements for the in-DLL session recorder ("Record" toggle)
and the complete entity dump that is the primary agent observability channel
into live PowerPoint sessions. Formalizes the design contract in
`docs/session-recorder-spec.md` (Phase 15 / v2.10.x). Closes the live-vs-harness
gap proven by the 2026-07-18 live verify of v2.9.0.
